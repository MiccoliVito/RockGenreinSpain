      <p>In the vibrant world of Spanish rock music, we set out on an adventure through data analysis. With SPARQL queries and our knowledge of Wikidata, we aimed to reveal the hidden stories that defined Spanish rock.
Our journey began with an astonishing revelation: Spain had a remarkable 1,931 music bands, with 66 belonging to the rock genre. This showcased the strong presence of rock music in the country.
Continuing our exploration, we took a virtual tour of Spanish concert halls through captivating map queries. Barcelona emerged as the music capital, boasting 9 music venues pulsating with the energy of unforgettable rock performances. Madrid followed closely behind with 8 amazing music venues, witnessing the power and passion of rock music.</p>
